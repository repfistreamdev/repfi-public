# Why RepFi is not a scam

This page exists because **healthy skepticism is good**. Here are concrete reasons to trust the model — and honest risks to understand.

---

## Red flags we deliberately avoid

| Common scam pattern | RepFi approach |
|---------------------|----------------|
| “Send crypto to unlock earnings” | **Never required.** Earning is free. |
| “Guaranteed 10% daily” | **No fixed returns.** Pay = verified reps × rate. |
| “Connect wallet & sign unknown tx” | **No wallet extension.** You paste a public address only. |
| “Recruit 5 friends to withdraw” | **No referral gate** on withdrawals. |
| Anonymous team, no product | **Live app** at repfi.stream + public docs repo. |
| Hidden treasury | **Pool balance public** on /pool. |

4. **Read docs** — full flow at repfi.stream/docs.

## How payouts are funded (no magic money)

Money does not appear from nothing:

1. **Donations** — users contribute USD/SOL to the pool wallet.
2. **$RepFi fees** — pump.fun creator fee share routes to the pool (when token is live).
3. **Athletes earn** only when the pool has funds to cover withdrawals.

If the pool is empty, withdrawals fail with a clear “pool” reason — not a silent rug.

---

## AI verification = less fraud

Unlike “click earn” apps:

- Reps require **visible body keypoints**
- **Elbow depth** and **body alignment** are checked
- Half reps and standing arm bends are rejected

Cheating is possible (e.g. fake video feed) but harder than button spam — and we continue tightening detection.

---

## Wallet safety

- We store your **public Solana address** for payouts.
- We **never** ask for:
  - Seed phrase / recovery words
  - Private key
  - “Approve all” wallet signatures on our site

If anyone claims to be RepFi support and asks for these — **it is a scam**.

---

## Token ($RepFi) transparency

- Token is optional for using the platform.
- Trading creates fees; fees feed the athlete pool.
- We do not promise token price appreciation.
- **DYOR** — crypto is volatile.
